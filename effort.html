Effort

Space Based Endeavours are focused on helping enable a secure, safe, sustainable & equitable infrastructure for outer space and other realms. In the early stages, revenue from aerospace activities will support healthcare initiatives until the healthcare initiatives themselves become self-sustaining.

Healthcare Specific Endeavours are going to be focused on leveraging the levels of autonomy in order to help enable a robust and high-quality healthcare systems across the solar system. This should be possible considering the arc of autonomy. Healthcare must be focused on care, without distorting the meaning of the word. And respecting the patient's privacy is of paramount importance.
